Not at all! Much the opposite actually! The techniques taught on the ‘Love your Birth’ courses come from the practice of hypnobirthing. Hypnobirthing refers to the ability to enter a state of deep relaxation where you are aware of sensations and your surroundings, but are not distracted by them. The tools and techniques you will be taught will help you to create anchors for that deep relaxation, that you can tap in to during labour. You will not be able to control the sensations within your body during labour, but with the use of these tools and techniques you will FEEL in control, because you will be able to understand the physiology.


Everyone’s perceptions of pain will be different. Because of this, we cannot guarantee a pain free experience. But from the knowledge you will gain and tools and techniques you can practice, this will reduce the element of fear. Fear is physiologically a big contributor to the level of pain experienced. It has been shown that people who use hypnobirthing techniques during labour are less likely to request the stronger forms of analgesia, and describe their births as powerful as opposed to painful.
